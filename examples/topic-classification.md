# Topic Classification Examples

## Objective

Assign each sentence to the most appropriate topic based on its primary subject.

### Available Topics

- Technology
- Finance
- Healthcare
- Education
- Sports
- Entertainment
- Business
- Travel
- Customer Support

---

## Example 1

**Sentence**

> Artificial intelligence is transforming the healthcare industry.

**Topic**

Healthcare

**Reason**

The sentence discusses the use of AI in healthcare, making Healthcare the primary topic.

---

## Example 2

**Sentence**

> The stock market closed higher after investors reacted positively to the earnings report.

**Topic**

Finance

**Reason**

The focus is on financial markets and investments.
